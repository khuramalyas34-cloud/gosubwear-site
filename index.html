PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 2026

At GOSUBWEAR, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you shop with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order via WhatsApp or our platform, we collect your name, phone number, and shipping address to fulfill your delivery.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for order processing, delivery, and customer support. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Security

We implement secure protocols to ensure your data is protected during the transaction process. Our Cash on Delivery (COD) model ensures no financial data (like credit cards) is stored on our servers.

4. Returns & Refunds

We offer a 7-day return assurance for manufacturing defects. Please contact our 24/7 support for assistance with returns.
